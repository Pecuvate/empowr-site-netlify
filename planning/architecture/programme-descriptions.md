## Drop-in Sessions

### Each1teach1: Skate Jam — Ages 15+

A welcoming, open session where skaters of all levels — from total beginners to those looking to sharpen their skills — come together to practise, learn, and connect. The name says it all: as you improve, you naturally support those around you. No pressure, no performance — just movement, community, and growth at your own pace.

---

### All Ages Roller Disco — Ages 5+

Our most open session — a joyful, energetic space where the whole community skates together. Suitable for all ages and all levels, the Roller Disco is about fun, freedom, and the feeling that comes from moving together. Whether you're stepping on skates for the first time or a familiar face, you'll leave having experienced something real.

---

## Structured Lessons

### Sk8 Skool for Kidz — Ages 5–15

A structured, progressive skating programme designed specifically for children and young people. Each session builds on the last — developing balance, coordination, confidence, and a genuine love of movement. Led by trained Empowr coaches, Sk8 Skool gives young people more than skating skills: it gives them a space to try, to grow, and to surprise themselves.

---

### SK8 Skool for All Ages — Ages 5+

The same progressive coaching approach as Sk8 Skool for Kidz, open to everyone. Whether you're a child finding your feet or an adult starting from scratch, this session meets you where you are and takes you further than you expected. Coaches create an environment where trying, falling, and getting back up is exactly the point.

## Courses

### Beginners Foundations — Ages 13+
### Beginners Outside — Ages 13+

A multi-session course that takes you from your very first steps on skates to a genuine foundation of skill and confidence. Across the programme you'll develop balance, movement, stopping, and control — guided by an Empowr coach throughout. By the end, skating won't feel like a challenge. It'll feel like yours.

The Beginners Outside course takes the same foundations into an outdoor setting — building adaptability and real-world confidence from the very start.

---

### Prep to Street Skating — Ages 13+ (Level 2)

The natural progression after Beginners. This Level 2 course takes your foundations outside and develops the technique, spatial awareness, and adaptability needed to skate confidently in real-world environments. For those who are ready to take their skating further and make it their own.

## Camps

### Roller Quad Camp — Ages 5–12
### Roller Skating Camp — Ages 5–15

An immersive holiday skating experience for children and young people. Over the course of the camp, participants develop their skating through structured sessions, games, and challenges designed to build skill, resilience, and connection. High-energy, inclusive, and genuinely memorable — the kind of experience that stays with you long after the skates come off.

---

## Advanced

### SYNKRON8 — Ages 15+

For skaters ready to go beyond the basics. SYNKRON8 is a two-hour session focused on routines, flow, and linking moves into sequences. It challenges participants to develop their own skating style while working within a shared structure — building coordination, creativity, and a deeper relationship with movement. If skating has become your thing, this is where it deepens.
