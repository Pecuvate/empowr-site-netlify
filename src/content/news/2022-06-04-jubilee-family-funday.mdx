On 4th June 2022, Empowr CIC partnered with The Somerville TRA and Barnes Wallis Community Centre to host a free Jubilee Family Funday for the local community. The event was made possible by funding from the Lewisham Tenants Fund — and the whole thing came together in under two weeks.

## The Day

True to our mission, every activity on the day was chosen with experiential learning in mind. We wanted to give the community meaningful, hands-on experiences, not just entertainment.

Activities included:

- Jumbo Games
- Skipping
- Hula Hoops
- Rubik's Cubes
- Bouncy Castle
- Taekwondo Demonstration
- Zumba
- RC Rock Crawling
- Face Painting

## The Numbers

- **400+ community members** attended
- **11 collaborators and companies** contributed to the day
- **100% of surveyed attendees** said it was the best family fun day in the area in years — and every single one expressed interest in future events

## Our Partners on the Day

Thank you to everyone who showed up for this community: Katy Paints Faces, Kayjayzin, Hevifooted UK, TFG Bouncycastles, E W TaeKwonDo, Honey Pot Food Shop, and Wonda Cakes.

Events like this are what Empowr is for. We look forward to doing it again.
