We're proud to announce Empowr CIC's first formal partnership with a school: **Ivydale Primary School** in Southeast London.

Through this collaboration, we're delivering after-school skating sessions for students — bringing the same community-first, experiential approach we use in all of our programmes directly into a school setting.

## Why This Matters

Getting children moving, learning new skills, and experiencing the joy of physical activity early in life has a lasting impact on their wellbeing. Skating in particular builds balance, coordination, persistence, and confidence — qualities that go far beyond the rink.

By introducing skating as an after-school activity, we hope to encourage children to explore new skills, stay active, and find a community they can grow with.

This partnership is also a proof of concept for Empowr's broader ambitions: to work with schools and organisations across Southeast London to deliver wellbeing programmes where young people already are, rather than expecting them to come to us.

We're grateful to Ivydale Primary School for opening their doors.
