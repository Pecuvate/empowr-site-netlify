On 4th February 2022, Empowr CIC held its very first session: a Skate Jam in Southeast London.

The idea was born from a simple observation. Our founder noticed that Southeast London had very few indoor spaces where people could roller skate. There was a gap — not just in facilities, but in community. Skating has a way of bringing people together, and that energy was exactly the kind of vehicle Empowr was built around.

The Skate Jam was the first proof of concept. A free, open, community event. The beginning of something that has since grown into a structured wellbeing organisation delivering programmes across multiple disciplines.

We invite anyone who wants to stay connected with Empowr to join our community WhatsApp group for updates and to connect with fellow members.
