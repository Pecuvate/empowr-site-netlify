Every summer, Empowr CIC runs a free 12-week street skating support programme for beginner street skaters in Southeast London.

The programme is straightforward: experienced London street skaters volunteer their time and expertise to help newcomers develop skills, build confidence, and find their footing on the streets. No cost to participants. No barrier to entry.

## What Participants Are Saying

The response from the community has been everything we hoped for.

> "Tuesday has officially become the best day of the week."

> "I Finally got to experience my first ever street skate. I absolutely adore what you are doing."

The dedication of our volunteers makes this possible, and the community has noticed. We're grateful to everyone who shows up — whether to skate or to help others skate.

## Join Us

Sessions run through the summer months. If you want to take part or stay updated, join our WhatsApp community group to get announcements and connect with fellow skaters.

This is what free, accessible wellbeing looks like in practice.
